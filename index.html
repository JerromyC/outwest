Making Lawn Care and Property Maintenance Easy and Worry-Free

At OutWest Landscaping, our mission is to elevate the beauty and value of every property we serve—delivering reliable, top-quality care that exceeds expectations at every step.

Lawn Maintenance

Keep your lawn healthy and beautiful with our expert lawn maintenance services. We offer regular mowing, trimming, and edging to ensure a neat and vibrant lawn all year round. Our team tailors care to meet the specific needs of your lawn, ensuring it thrives through every season.

Landscaping

Transform your outdoor space with professional landscaping services. From vibrant gardens to custom hardscaping, we design and install solutions that enhance your yard’s beauty and curb appeal. Let our team bring your landscape vision to life with creative, personalized care.

Sod Install

Get a lush, green lawn fast with our sod installation. We prepare your soil and expertly lay sod for quick establishment, giving you a beautiful lawn right away.

Flowerbed Maintenance

Keep your flowerbeds vibrant with our maintenance service, including weeding, mulching, pruning, and seasonal plantings for long-lasting beauty.

Aeration

Improve lawn health with aeration, which reduces soil compaction and helps water, air, and nutrients reach the roots for a stronger, greener lawn.
